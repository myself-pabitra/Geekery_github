About us!

Creativity is our hallmark but producing a super-creative, super-intriguing video takes a lot of collaborative effort and is a complete team play
So we, at You Media believe to have a symbiotic relationship with our clients so they know exactly how their project is being treated and at what stage it is at all times with our Live Dashboards

Why do we do this?

If we encounter a question never asked before, the answer needs to be unique as well. So it is not enough that we build designs that function, are usable, and understandable, but also bring joy, excitement, pleasure, fun and yes, beauty to people's lives. Essentially, we are reinventing how good design is coupled with a delightful client experience, at scale

The idea here is not to just provide standard video marketing plans to our clients. We study our clients, their business type, and their target audience and accordingly suggest the most appropriate video marketing campaign to accelerate the business. Every pitch/offering from You Media is based on detailed research and fully personalised to our client's needs
